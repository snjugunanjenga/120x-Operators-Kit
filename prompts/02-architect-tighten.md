# Prompt 02 — Architect Tighten

**Use with:** The same Architect (Claude Chat, ChatGPT, etc.) immediately after Prompt 01.

**When to use:** When the first-draft pack is too generic, too verbose, or includes AI-style filler. Run this once to tighten the pack into something a Builder can actually execute against.

**What it does:** Forces the Architect to remove generic AI advice, preserve project-specific facts, and stop the Builder from being asked to make broad product decisions.

---

## The Prompt

```text
Now tighten this into a Builder-ready version.

For each file, make the content concise, specific, and operational.
Remove generic AI advice.
Preserve project-specific facts, commitments, risks, and open questions.
Make sure the Builder is not asked to make broad product decisions.
```
